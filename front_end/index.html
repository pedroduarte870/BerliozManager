About Berlioz Manager

There are already several applications for managing schools but none resolve well the specifics of a music school. Virtually none of the existing applications to manage schools have the functionality to manage auditions or concerts. This is a specific need for a school of music over a school year may have to organize tens or hundreds of auditions and concerts.

This application was inserted into the integrated project of Languages and Knowledge Processing profile in computer engineering course at the University of Minho. The project presentation can be found on instructor page.
